About Bailee

I'm a wanna-be full-stack web developer with a focus on front-end development. In July, I'll receive a certificate in coding from the University of Texas at Austin bootcamp. With degrees in business marketing and human biology, my passion for and ability to learn vastly different and complex material defines who I am and how I approach every opportunity - if I don't have an answer today, I am confident I'll know it tomorrow. I fell in love with coding during a computation biology class and in the midst of applying to medical school. I'm particularly drawn to the creative problem solving and expression that coding offers in a data-driven setting. I'm an independent thinker, but an enthusiastic collaborator, and currently seeking an entry-level development position in Austin, Texas.

Fortunate enough to be surrounding by love (re: my husband and our cat) and the warmth of ATX. We enjoy a healthy dose of caffiene and bi-weekly thai food.

Now reading: "Tears of Autumn" by Charles McCarry
Currently on a string cheese and Yasso frozen yogurt bar kick.
Last movie watched: Lord of the Rings: Return of the King.

I graduated with a BBA in Marketing from the University of Oklahoma in 2016 and will graduate, again!, in the spring of 2020 with a BS in Human Biology from the University of Texas in Austin.
